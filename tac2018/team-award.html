ABOUT THE TEAM AWARD

Is your team part of Agile / DevOps transformation journey? Are you able to bring agility in the product you work for as a team? How did you achieve it? How did you adopt and balance among practices and tools based upon Agile values and principles, Engineering practices, DevOps practices?

Are you feeling proud about your teams agility?

Do your customers tell you that you are align with their business?

Do you hear wow when you do your product demo?

TAC2018 brings you the right platform to showcase your work model in front of well renowned experts & professionals round the globe.

Nominate your team for this prestigious Award and let the world know about your success and get recognized globally.

How it's works

  • Nomination Team
  • 25th July 2018

  • Submit Your Details

  • 1st August 2018

  • Round 1 - Screening

    Based upon the information provided screening will be done by the judges.

  • Top 6 team will be choosen for next round and informed.

  • 4th August 2018

  • Round 2 - Face to of face evaluation

    Face to face discussion (Physical presence or Skype video call) will be scheduled with the board of Judges.

  • Top 3 team will be choosen for final round.

  • 11th August 2018

  • Round 3 - Present in front of Conference participants

    Judges and audience will questions. Based upon Voting by the Audience and Marked given by the Judges.

  • Winner

    1st Runner and 2nd Runner will be declared.
