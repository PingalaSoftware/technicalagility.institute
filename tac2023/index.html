ABOUT THE EVENT

TAC2022 is the fifth edition of world's first Technical Agility themed conference, which will be virtual this year amid of the COVID 19 effect. After the grand success of TAC2018, TAC2019, TAC2020 (Virtual edition) & TAC2021 (Virtual edition) and the positive and continuous responses from different peoples across the globe, we are all ready to rock again this year.

Experience the Unexperienced – Let’s break the silos! Why not listen and witness the story of those unsung heroes, such teams who under the guidance and supervision of Agile Experts, leaders, and coaches create history, bring up something exciting, innovative and challenging outcomes. TAC2022 is going to set the platform for all such unknown heroes of different teams from different organizations to come, join and share their real time success journey / stories with us during the 3 days of conference. Join us to be a part of the super exciting 5th edition of Technical Agility Conference.

TAC2021 - The fourth edition of World's first Technical Agility Conference was held virtually amid of the COVID effect and witnessed a remarkable response from all over the globe. The immense support and swift responses from different industries and organizations made it fourth in a row as one of the most successful conference of the year 2021 amid of COVID effect. More than 750+ participants from 250+ different organizations and 30+ speakers shared their real time experience and rich expertise on different topics and tracks virtually. With the same spirit and energy we are all set to gear up the platform for TAC2022.

The vision is to create a platform for individuals to come, share, learn and experience from the experts & with the experts across the globe under one roof.

In today’s environment, everyone is seeking for the luxury of stability but very few of the organizations succeed to achieve it. Today’s market is completely volatile and unpredictable where you never know what’s gonna happen next and the common question across all the mind is “How do I do this?”

The most common reason why organizations are struggling to sustain their existence in market is because they are lacking the Business Agility. They struggle to achieve Business Agility as for the limitations in their product development & delivery such as legacy product, old technologies, unable to scale and many such other reasons. In a nutshell, there is no Technical Agility in the organization!

Bringing Agility into the products, leads to Technical Agility and can be achieved through a combination of some well-known & widely adopted practices such as Agile/ Lean values and principles, Agile engineering practices and DevOps practices along with many tools & technologies that support these practices.

But prior to that, what actually it means to be a customer driven organization? How to make your business ready to thrive in this competitive & volatile market? How to come out of the real world challenges and issues facing in today’s unpredictable environment?

TAC2022 will help you understand the prospects and how to overcome any such situations with real time experiences and examples from well renowned industry experts & practitioners. The 3 days of conference will cover all the aspects related to how to achieve Technical Agility and maximize the profit of your organization as well as on individual level.

Categories

We are going to bring you experience in 3 major categories. In each category, presenters will cover their journey (success and failure) across the 3 pillars.

Team Agility


  • We want to bring the best breed of agile development teams, who can narrate their stories about their transformation stories and
  • how they added value to their business
  • how they became a great team who produce results
  • how they were able to Collaborate within and across teams
  • how they produced stability to what they are building
  • how they ensured quality for what they developed
  • how they ensured seamless customer delivery and upgrade
  • how the team learnt together

Program Agility

  • under this theme, we will bring you how multiple teams (team of teams) came together to build a great product or solve a customer problem. We will get to learn from them on bringing up a agile programs,
  • what kind of problem they faced to bring, agility to the program
  • how did they solve the problems
  • What methodologies they have applied to bring more order to the system
  • How were they organized
  • Methodologies followed
  • Lesson learnt
  • How did they enable the business
  • How did they ensure quality of the program
  • The delivery mechanisms they have adopted
  • How did they manage stakeholders
  • Many more

Organization Agility


  • The journey get even more exciting when we look at organization agility as a whole. Even though it is such a desirable state many / majority of the organizations are just initiated the journey, there goals are still evolving. We will bring you some industry leading organizations who have done exemplary improvement in the organization agility.
  • What is organization agility means to them
  • How did they design for success
  • Who are the key stake holders
  • Who do they measure their agility
  • What have not worked for them and what have worked for them
  • Many more


You will also get to interact with each of these presenters and ask questions. We are sure you will be able to get wonderful insights and learning from this event. We want to ensure that you get noting less than the best.
