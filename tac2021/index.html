ABOUT THE EVENT

TAC2021 is the fourth edition of world's first Technical Agility themed conference which will be virtual this year amid of the COVID 19 effect. After the grand success of TAC2018, TAC2019 & TAC2020 (Virtual edition) and the positive and continuous responses from different peoples across the globe, we are all ready to rock again this year.

TAC2020 - The third edition of World's first Technical Agility Conference was held virtually amid of the COVID effect and witnessed a remarkable response from all over the globe. The immense support and swift responses from different industries and organizations made it third in a row as one of the most successful conference of the year 2020 amid of COVID effect. More than 750+ participants from 250+ different organizations and 30+ speakers shared their real time experience and rich expertise on different topics and tracks virtually. With the same spirit and energy we are all set to gear up the platform for TAC2021.

The vision is to create a platform for individuals to come, share, learn and experience from the experts & with the experts across the globe under one roof.

In today’s environment, everyone is seeking for the luxury of stability but very few of the organizations succeed to achieve it. Today’s market is completely volatile and unpredictable where you never know what’s gonna happen next and the common question across all the mind is “How do I do this?”

The most common reason why organizations are struggling to sustain their existence in market is because they are lacking the Business Agility. They struggle to achieve Business Agility as for the limitations in their product development & delivery such as legacy product, old technologies, unable to scale and many such other reasons. In a nutshell, there is no Technical Agility in the organization!

Bringing Agility into the products, leads to Technical Agility and can be achieved through a combination of some well-known & widely adopted practices such as Agile/ Lean values and principles, Agile engineering practices and DevOps practices along with many tools & technologies that support these practices.

But prior to that, what actually it means to be a customer driven organization? How to make your business ready to thrive in this competitive & volatile market? How to come out of the real world challenges and issues facing in today’s unpredictable environment?

TAC2021 will help you understand the prospects and how to overcome any such situations with real time experiences and examples from well renowned industry experts & practitioners. The 3 days of conference will cover all the aspects related to how to achieve Technical Agility and maximize the profit of your organization as well as on individual level.

Keynote Speakers


Karl Scotland

Agile Transformation Services Practice Manager at TEKsystems Global Services
Unwrapping the Agile Present
Abstract -

Agile Transformations are often treated as projects, with grand plans to implement blueprints or new ways of working, with ambitious deadlines. This approach assumes that the result of an Agile Transformation can be knowable in advance and that all the information and expertise is already in place. However, an organisational transformation, just like product development, is a process of knowledge discovery and learning, where we need to make small changes, sense and respond. Thus rather than looking into the future to make our plans, we should look to the present, understand the current situation and explore adjacent possibilities. This talk will examine what it means to realise an Agile Transformation in an agile way, and how that can be achieved.

Ardita Karaj

President at Industrial Logic Canada
You can’t buy your organization’s handbook on Amazon
Abstract -

Leading an organization is not easy. You want to always be learning, improving and innovating. We have been told since young that we need to learn from the success of others. So we go and search what Google, Facebook, Microsoft, Apple… and all the big successful companies do. We tell our people that we want to do what they are doing and achieve what they are achieving.

But have we ever stopped to think “Whom did these big successful companies learn from?” Did they hire someone that gave them ideas that nobody had heard of before? Did they read a book that revealed secrets? Did they pay a lot of money to a consulting company that created a framework just for them and told their employees what culture they needed to have?

You know none of this is true. So let’s look into how to learn from our own people and lessons.

Michael K Sahota

Speaker, Trainer, Consultant & Author on Evolutionary Leadership at SHIFT314
The SHIFT314 Evolutionary Leadership Framework (SELF): Scaling Excellence through People
Abstract -

For the last 10 years the world of Agile Transformation has been dominated by scaling frameworks such as SAFe, LeSS, Spotify Model and more recently under the code word “New Ways of Working”. Even though Agile is supposed to be People over process, the focus is on process and structures. What happens when we focus on scaling through people? The SHIFT314 Evolutionary Leadership Framework (SELF™) provides the maps, models, principles and tools needed to scale excellence. The best part is that it operates at the level of people - the culture and leadership - it is created to amplify the success of your favourite scaling framework.

We cover the highlights and the foundation of the SELF from the book “Leading Beyond Change: A Practical Guide to Evolving Business Agility”. The SELF can be understood as a way to rethink Agile at an organizational level and it’s application to all new ways of working: Agile, Digital, Lean, etc. At the core is an advanced approach and an integrated understanding of culture, leadership and organizational change. Culture shift comes with the evolution of people - that’s what Evolutionary Leadership is all about: it’s the natural successor of Servant Leadership where individual evolution is the fundamental unit of real change.

Dave Snowden

Founder and Chief Scientific Officer at Cognitive Edge
Agile Leadership or Leadership in Agile
Abstract -

Leadership in, by and through Agile

What is the role of leadership in a modern organisation? At a senior level the focus is more on co-ordination and distributing decision making but this changes in teams and at middle management level. There are no context free approaches or lists of leadership characteristics that apply in all circumstances. This presentation will examine both the principles of leadership through a lens of complexity and will also look at new methods and models of organisation.

The new normal is a Myth! Prepare for the “never normal” with Anti-Fragile and Business Agility
Abstract -

As per the International Monetary Fund (IMF) - “The economic impact of COVID-19 is unlike anything we have seen since the great depression of the 1930s”. There are so many claims that we have entered the new normal - the normal of remote work, changes in business strategies, supplier chains, recruitment patterns and so on. The fact is - it is never going to be normal, and it never was. Not just Agile, but the Anti-Fragile will survive. Not just specific functions of the business, but Business Agility is required to continue to thrive in the ever uncertain world. Join this session to understand the never normal through the experience of an organization agility coach.
